Hanging Rock and North Carolina's Pillars

Hope Stremcha MAP671

Pillars extracted from the Domestic and Antarctic Names (State and Topical Gazetteer) published by the United States Board on Geographic Namkes. (geonames.usgs.gov) Data accessed August, 2018. Contours were extracted from a USGS NED 1/3 arc-second raster digital elevation model of the Danbury, NC area. This data can be accessed at the USGS 3D Elevation Program (usgs.gov)

Tools used in this project

  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled.
  • GDAL and QGIS 2.18.21 to convert raster elevation layer and create contours.
